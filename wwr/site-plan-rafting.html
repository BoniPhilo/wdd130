Overview

Purpose

The primary purpose of the "Rapid Adventures" website is to provide outdoor enthusiasts with comprehensive information about whitewater rafting adventures, allowing users to book trips, understand safety measures, and get acquainted with various trip levels and options. Through intuitive navigation and vibrant visuals, the site aims to evoke the thrill of rafting while ensuring users feel informed and safe about their upcoming adventure.

Audience

Who are the target customers?
Our target customers include:
- Adventurous Individuals: Adults between the ages of 18 and 50 who seek thrill and adventure.
- Families: Parents with children above 10 looking for a bonding experience.
- Tourists: Visitors wanting a unique regional experience.
Describe their life (or business) situation:
- Adventurous Individuals: Professionals or students seeking adventure during vacations.
- Families: Middle to upper-middle-class families with a recreational budget.
- Tourists: Visitors from other regions or countries.
What do they want?
- Adventurous Individuals: Thrilling experiences.
- Families: Safe yet exciting adventures.
- Tourists: A signature regional experience.
What are their needs that aren't being met?
Tailor-made experiences with clear safety guidelines.
How will my audience usually access my site?
Primarily via mobile devices but also through desktops or laptops for detailed planning.
